Different textiles can do different things with negative space. For [[Textiles/Embroidery/index|Embroidery]] an unembroidered area can represent negative space. For [[Textiles/Sewing/index|Sewing]], a solid color can represent negative space if the piece otherwise uses colorful prints. [[Yarn Crafts]] and [[Textiles/Needle Felting/index|Needle Felting]] are capable of creating physical holes of different sizes.
