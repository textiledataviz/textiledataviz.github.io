Reading logs are common across several crafts. For instance, crocheting a [[Granny square]] in colors that match the cover of each book you read, or embroidering a design in different colors reflecting other data about your reading habits (e.g. genre, physical/audiobook/ebook, etc.) When reading in an academic context, "books" may make less sense than pages or words, and type of reading (e.g. academic article, novel, lab manual, popular non-fiction).
