Claudia Berger and Gabriella Evergreen's [Pockets of Information](https://storymaps.arcgis.com/stories/9f1d23f02fa8483f884c1b6d20bf0762) combines data with [[Garment Sewing]] and [[Surface Embroidery]], natural dyes, as well as mixed media work, to tell a story about New York City in 2124, facing the impact of climate change. It primarily draws on [[Civic Data]] and includes an embroidered speculative map on the back. The project website includes a detailed write-up of the data and the narrative.
