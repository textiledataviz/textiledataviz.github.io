![A gold and red striped crochet scarf](batter-up.jpg)

[Dr. Laura Guertin](https://journeysofdrg.org/) used [[Sports Data]] to [[Textiles/Crochet/index|crochet]] a scarf showing the number of batters for each team during the Phillies (red) / Pirates (yellow) game on May 18, 2025. She has a [detailed write-up of her process](https://journeysofdrg.org/2025/05/19/data-viz-crochet-batter-up/) and the adventures of trying to keep up with the data live. She made a [second scarf](https://journeysofdrg.org/2025/07/18/data-viz-crochet-batter-up-v2/) for the July 11, 2025 baseball game between the Red Sox (red) Tampa Bay Rays (blue).

This is similar to [[The Season Scarf]], but focusing on a single game rather than the entire season, and using crochet rather than knitting.
