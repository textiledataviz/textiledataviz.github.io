[Sarah Kay Miller](https://sarahkaymiller.com/) knit a [pair of socks](https://bsky.app/profile/thesarahkaym.bsky.social/post/3lcvjb4etck2q) representing the electoral college voting data from the 2020 election (a type of [[Civic Data]]).

The squares representing the votes are similar to the style of "typical" data visualization, using color and quantity.
