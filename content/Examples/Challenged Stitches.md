The [Challenged Stitches quilt](https://journeysofdrg.org/2025/12/10/challenged-stitches-a-quilt-with-banned-book-data/) by [Dr. Laura Guertin](https://journeysofdrg.org/) uses [[Organizational Data]] from the American Library Association showing the number of books challenged each year. The design recreates a standard data visualization bar chart.
