[Rebecca Sutton Koeser](https://rlskoeser.github.io/) and [Gissoo Doroudian](https://www.gisdor.com/) created a [weaving of references in the first half of Chapter 1 of Jacques Derrida’s *de la Grammatologie* (1967)](https://startwords.cdh.princeton.edu/issues/1/data-beyond-vision/#weaving). In this [[Textiles/Weaving/index|weaving]], they use different color (and texture, perhaps more significantly than color) to represent different kinds of references. The organization of the data is chronological. There is a [digital presentation of the weaving](https://startwords.cdh.princeton.edu/issues/1/weaving-as-interface/) that annotates the textile with each reference.
