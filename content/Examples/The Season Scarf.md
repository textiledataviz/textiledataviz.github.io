[Available on Ravelry](https://www.ravelry.com/patterns/library/the-season-scarf), this pattern offers instructions for how to [[Textiles/Knitting/index|knit]] a simple striped scarf covering 162 games of a Major League Baseball team's season ([[Sports Data]], with different colors representing wins and losses.

This is similar to [[Batter Up]], but focusing on the whole season rather than a single game, and using knitting rather than crochet.
