**Things tracked**

- Page length tracked as quilt border

- Book rating tracked as quilt border on all 4 sides

- Other quilt border ideas: medium (audio, Kindle, paperback, hardback); source (library, secondhand, gift, bookstore)

- Genre as quilt block, but implementation uses book cover colors instead of the colors marked in the pattern

  

  

**Pink** - Romance

**Red** - Horror

**Red-Orange** - Nonfiction

**Orange** - Historical Fiction

**Yellow-Orange** - Contemporary

**Yellow** - Poetry

**Yellow-Green** - Magical Realism

**Green** - Fantasy

**Blue-Green** - Story Collection

**Blue** - Mystery / Adventure

**Blue-Purple** - Thriller

**Purple** - Literary Fic

**Magenta** - Graphic Novel

**Brown** - Biography / Memoir

**Dark Gray** - SciFi

**Light Gray** – Classic
