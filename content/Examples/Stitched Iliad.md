In 2019, [Silvie Kilgallon](https://www.tumblr.com/theclassicistblog) set out to stitch the 24 books of *The Iliad*, one [[Cross-Stitch]] per letter of the text. Each letter was mapped to a different color of embroidery thread, and the intention was to substitute one letter's red-color with a unique blue-color every time she started a new book. This project represents [[Data from Text]]. She fully stitched the first book (shown above), and was on the third book when she stopped posting updates.

There's [more information about the project here](https://stitchediliad.tumblr.com/TheApology).
