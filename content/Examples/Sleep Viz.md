From 2024-present, [Pam Lach](https://bsky.app/profile/plach.bsky.social) has created [data visualizations of her sleep and insomnia](https://bsky.app/profile/plach.bsky.social/post/3mez6aotn7c2r), through a spiral of different-colored Z shapes, some with beads. This [[Surface Embroidery]] uses color and beads to represent different data.

Before Pam came up with this brilliant representation of the idea, I was noodling around with embroidery and insomnia, but I had a lot of trouble with the layout (below). I also abandoned the project in 2024 as my life radically changed, and the insomnia that was the driving motivation for doing the project disappeared. There's a [longer write-up of all that here](https://quinndaedal.com/textiles/2024-in-insomnia/).
