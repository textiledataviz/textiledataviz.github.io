[Seung Lee](https://bsky.app/profile/lagomorpho.com) created blankets for both of his children, tracking their sleep over the first year of their lives. Each row represents a single day, and each stitch represents six minutes spent awake (blue/pink) or asleep (gray).

This piece uses [[Data/Created Data/Personal Data/index|Personal Data]] and  [[Textiles/Knitting/index|Knitting]] (with color / organization to represent the data). There's a more detailed [writeup here](https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2666389920300465).
