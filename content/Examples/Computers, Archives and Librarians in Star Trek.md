An early textile data visualization experiment with a [[Rigid heddle loom]], I set up data about the different series associations of *Star Trek* novels (e.g. Original Series, Next Generation, DS9, Voyager, etc.) on the warp, and the weft is made up of one yarn representing the series of the book, and then a black yarn if a "computer" was mentioned, a thick blue yarn if a "librarian" was mentioned, and a black and white yarn if an "archive" was mentioned. Every book included a reference to the "computer"; some included a librarian or archive as well.  The organization of the data is in order of publication; I had intended to include all books in the [[Weaving|weaving]], but ran out of warp after the TOS books.

I have a [detailed write-up of the weaving](https://quinndaedal.com/textiles/computers-archives-librarians-star-trek/) along with a link to the associated paper that the data analysis was originally for.
