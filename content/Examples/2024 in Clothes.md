By the time my 2024 ended, I had a very different life than when I started the year. That new life included several embroidery classes. But at the start of the year, I had a goal of doing a little embroidery every day, and I set out to capture the outfits my 6-year-old and I wore every day.

This [[Surface Embroidery|embroidery]] used [[Clothes]] data, and shape to represent the months as well as the nature of the outfit in question. Filled-in shapes represented dresses, line art represented separates.

There's a [more detailed write-up](https://quinndaedal.com/textiles/2024-in-clothes/) of this piece.
