Olivia Johnson's [Women's Work](https://womens-work.info/) is a hybrid digital/physical series that uses [[Cross-Stitch]] to render "standard" data visualizations (e.g. heat maps, radial charts, pie charts) relating to discrimination against women -- particularly marginalized women -- in the workplace. The cross-stitch pieces are overlaid with metadata in the online presentation of the series.

This series uses [[Civic Data]], along with color, shape, size, and proportion to show the data.
