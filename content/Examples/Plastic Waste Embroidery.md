In 2021, [Nithya Subramanian](https://www.poppadomco.com/) was working on [[Surface Embroidery]] data. visualization of plastic waste that washes up on beaches. On the left, the embroidery uses gray [[Kantha]] stitches to represent individual cigarette butts found on 10 km of beach in Germany ([[Data/Created Data/index|created data]]).
