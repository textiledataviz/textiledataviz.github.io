![What looks like a quilt, but is actually a cross-stitch project](cross-stitch-book-quilt.jpg)

 [Sirithré]( [https://sirithre.com/](https://sirithre.com/)) designed a [pattern for a cross-stitch book quilt]([https://www.etsy.com/listing/4362461271/book-tracking-quilt-cross-stitch-pattern](https://www.etsy.com/listing/4362461271/book-tracking-quilt-cross-stitch-pattern). There's a [detailed video about the design process]( [https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sLFvxX8TeP4](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=sLFvxX8TeP4)) as well. It uses [[color]], [[shape]], and [[quantity]] to represent the [[Reading Logs]] as [[Cross-Stitch]]. 

The piece captures:

* Page length in the border of the quilt pieces
* Book rating in the border of the quilt pieces
* Genre mapped to the quilt block designs (though this implementation uses book cover colors instead)
* Other data suggestions include medium (audio, Kindle, paperback, hardback) and source (library, secondhand, gift, bookstore)
