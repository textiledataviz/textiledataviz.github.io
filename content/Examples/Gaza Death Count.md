Artist [Maya Amer](https://www.mayaamerdesign.com/) [designed an embroidery](https://hyperallergic.com/palestinian-artists-digital-embroidery-illustrates-gaza-death-toll/) for the collar of a traditional Palestinian garment that depicts the death count in Palestine as of Oct 31, 2023, with a metadata key mapping the stitches to women, men, girls, boys, and unidentified victims. 

This visualization uses color and [[Civic Data]] (reported death tolls).
