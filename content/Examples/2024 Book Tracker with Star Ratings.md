[Roseanna Pendlebury](https://linktr.ee/roseanna.pendlebury) had a 2024 book tracker ([thread on Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/chloroformtea.bsky.social/post/3kix4jhldvz2b)) where she used [[Surface Embroidery]] to make a sketch version of the covers of the books she read as she read them, along with a star rating. This is an example of [[Reading Logs]], and uses the properties of organization (chronological order) and quantity (star ratings).
