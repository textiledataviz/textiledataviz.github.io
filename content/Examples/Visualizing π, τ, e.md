Danièle Gibney wrote about her foray into textile data visualization in [Crossing into Dataviz](https://nightingaledvs.com/crossing-into-datavis/), and described her first project visualizing the first 80 digits of mathematical constants π, τ and e.

This visualization uses something akin to [[Scientific Data]] along with color and organization (representing the digits in order).
