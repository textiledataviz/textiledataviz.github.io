![A purple-gray and a brown-green-gray rigid heddle woven scarf](gerunds-saliva.jpg)

This pair of [[Rigid heddle loom]] [[Textiles/Weaving/index|weavings]] captures the first 3.5 days of text chats between me and my girlfriend, a form of [[Interactions|Interaction Data]]. I wove the data twice: once for my girlfriend (her messages in brown/green, mine in gray) and once for me (my messages in purple, hers in gray). The warp is set up using the proportion of messages from her vs. from me (roughly 2:1).  Every message is represented by one weft yarn. Images that were exchanged are represented by [[Leno]] (a finger-manipulated warp technique similar to the weaving equivalent of stitches). A small segment of [[Spanish Lace]] represents shifting from the dating app platform to Signal.

There is a more detailed description in the [zine for these weavings](https://quinndaedal.com/textiles/gerunds-saliva/).
