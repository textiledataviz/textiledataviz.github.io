[Trish Greenhalgh](https://bsky.app/profile/trishgreenhalgh.bsky.social) used [[Textiles/Knitting/index|Knitting]] to create a [cardigan called "Teams"](https://bsky.app/profile/trishgreenhalgh.bsky.social/post/3lbi5ewrduk2j) where every square is a meeting that could have been an email. The colors and designs are not mapped to any kind specific data (e.g. who called the meeting, meeting duration, etc.), but it still uses quantity to represent data points.
