![The back of a shirt, with rows of hourglass shapes filled with French knots and chain stitches in brown and purple](excommunication.jpg)

For this [[Surface Embroidery|embroidery]], I worked out how many text messages and words of email my ex and I exchanged every day of 2025, the year following our divorce. 50 words of email are represented by one chain stitch; one text message is represented by a French knot. His color is brown, mine is purple. Each day is contained in a single [Japanese darning stitch](https://rsnstitchbank.org/stitch/japanese-darning-stitch) which looks like an hourglass. This piece uses organization, color, and quantity as its properties.
