My girlfriend and I visited her parents for a few days in May 2025, and I kept track of the different ways that her mother introduced me to her friends and crocheted it into a [[Granny square]], which inherently has properties similar to a pie chart (albeit with a fixed count). Her mother also [[Textiles/Crochet/index|crochets]], so that textile method felt most fitting. I used gray yarn to make the necessary stitch cluster counts for the granny square work. This piece uses [[color]] as well as [[quantity]] as properties, and draws on [[Interactions|interaction data]].

(I made this granny square in about 10 minutes on the train on the day of the *Data Visualization with Textiles* showcase, after I lost the original one in my bag.)
