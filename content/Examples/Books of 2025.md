Debi's [book tracker embroidery](https://bsky.app/profile/bookwyrmsbookshelf.bsky.social/post/3m2kv6ll3os2b) for 2025 is done in [[Surface Embroidery]] and uses shape to convey different properties using color. Her book rating is the first horizontal stripe on each book spine, and the type of book (adult, young adult, middle grade) is the second horizontal stripe on the book spine. The genre(s) fill in the rest of the book spine. This embroidery uses [[Reading Logs]]
