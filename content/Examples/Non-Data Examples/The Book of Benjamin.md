[Ben Millett](https://benmillett.net/) created [The Book of Benjamin](https://benmillett.net/blog/the-book-of-benjamin) with inspiration from [The Book of Kells](https://www.tcd.ie/library/research-collections/book-of-kells.php), telling part of his life story using lyrics from songs important to him. It doesn't map to data as such, but it uses embroidery and text to tell a story.
