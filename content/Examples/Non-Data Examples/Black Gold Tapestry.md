Sandra M Sawatzky created the [Black Gold Tapestry](https://www.theblackgoldtapestry.com/) to tell a story about the history of oil, power, and energy transition, in the style of the [Bayeux Tapestry](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Bayeux_Tapestry). The piece uses [[Surface Embroidery]].
