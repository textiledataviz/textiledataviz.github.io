[*Drowning at Sea* by Elena Colón-Marrero](https://openbooks.library.umass.edu/unseen-labor-exhibit/chapter/elena-colon-marrero/) uses [[Surface Embroidery]] to show Colón-Marrero's "time spent learning how to describe software materials while working at a computing museum. No matter what I did, suggested, or wrote guidelines for there was always another person around to tell me to consider other schemas, taxonomies, and guidelines."

To add a data angle to this piece (even if it'd necessarily be somewhat vague and impressionistic), one could use [[size]] and scale the text of the words based on how often it was recommended, or add some [[quantity]] of bubbles to represent that number.
