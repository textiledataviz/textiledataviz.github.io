[Tabitha Arnold](https://www.tabithaarnold.com/) uses [[Punch-Needle Embroidery]] to create [large-scale tapestries](https://www.tabithaarnold.com/work) with themes of labor and working-class life. While these tapestries do not include data, one could imagine, for instance, using [[quantity]] of figures or [[color]] to represent data about union membership, workplace injuries, or other [[Civic Data]].
