[Tia Curtis](https://www.tiacurtisquilts.com/) is a quilter who uses topography maps ([[Geospatial Data]]) as inspiration for her [[Quilting]]. She has written up a [tutorial on topographic quilting](https://tiacurtisquilts.blogspot.com/2015/02/topography-quilting-tutorial.html), which doesn't get into maps as data as much as visual elements, but one can imagine linking topographic quilting to another related data set about a place.
