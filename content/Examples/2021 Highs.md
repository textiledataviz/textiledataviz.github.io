![A cross-stitch of a bookshelf with the books representing the high temperatures for the day](2021-highs.jpg)

[2021 Highs by Jeanette Kalchik](https://openbooks.library.umass.edu/unseen-labor-exhibit/chapter/jeanette-kalchik/) uses [[Cross-Stitch]] to show a bookshelf with the highs for Fremont, CA ([[Temperature Data]]) for January-November 2021. The call number at the top of the bookshelf represents Physics — Meteorology. Climatology — Climatology and weather — Geographic divisions — United States — California — Fremont.

The piece uses [[color]] to represent the temperature, [[organization]] to separate the months (one shelf per month, in chronological order), and [[negative space]] to make a point about the unfinished work of metadata.
