[Erica Lord](https://ericalord.com/home.html)'s series *[The Codes We Carry: Beadwork as DNA](https://ericalord.com/section/522353-Beadwork%20as%20DNA%20Data.html)* recreates computer-generated DNA/RNA microarray visualizations using a traditional Indigenous glass beading craft (a form of [[Textiles/Weaving/index|weaving]]), which takes the form of Alaskan Athabaskan baby belts / burden straps. Each bead represents a data point. The test used for the original computer visualization ([[Scientific Data]]) detects the presence and progression of a disease, and she chose diseases that disproportionately affect Native populations.
