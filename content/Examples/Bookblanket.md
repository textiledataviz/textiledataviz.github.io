The [#bookblanket hashtag on Bluesky](https://bsky.app/hashtag/bookblanket) features granny squares and other [[Textiles/Crochet/index|Crochet]] shapes that textile artists create as part of making a blanket out of their [[Reading Logs]]. Each granny square is one book / data point, using color not to convey some other data, but as an abstract representation of the book cover.
