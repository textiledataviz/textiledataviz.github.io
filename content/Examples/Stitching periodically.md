![A series of cross-stitched periodic tables focusing on different properties](stitching-periodically.jpg)

Danièle Gibney wrote about her foray into textile data visualization in [Crossing into Dataviz](https://nightingaledvs.com/crossing-into-datavis/), which includes a long description of her project doing [[Cross-Stitch]] for different properties of periodic tables, along with how doing this helped her better understand the properties of the elements.

This visualizations use [[Scientific Data]] along with color and organization. It recreates the style and conventions of periodic tables, a form of standard visualization.
