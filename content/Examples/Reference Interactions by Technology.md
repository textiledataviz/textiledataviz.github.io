![A flat sheet of machine knitting with different color stripes](reference-interactions.jpg)

[Franklin Sayre](https://franklinsayre.com/) used [[Machine Knitting]] to generate a flat knit sheet of [makerspace reference stats](https://bsky.app/profile/franklinsayre.bsky.social/post/3l26qb3u4zs2q) from 2022-2024. Each row represents 300 interactions. Red is tour, green is cutting machine, turquoise is 3D scanning and printing. This piece uses color and quantity to represent the data.
