The [Tatter Textile Library](https://tatter.org/) organized a [community project to stitch a banner](https://www.instagram.com/p/DHoIRQhpfpc/?igsh=QkFKZWx3TGdkUw%3D%3D) memorializing the victims of the 1911 [Triangle Shirtwaist Factory fire](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Triangle_Shirtwaist_Factory_fire). Each of the 146 victims had their own piece of the banner with their name, and sometimes biographical details.

This data visualization uses [[Civic Data]] (records of victims) and text, along with [[Machine Embroidery]] and [[Surface Embroidery]].

![An in-progress image of the banner with more names and a spreadsheet in the background](triangle-shirtwaist-progress.jpg)
