Kantha is a Bengali craft tradition involving layered fabric and a dense running stitch. The word is used for both the stitch and the resulting cloth. It can be a form of mending, much like [[Sashiko]]. 

## Resources and Tutorials
* [From Humble Beginnings: The Kantha Work of West Bengal](https://thefabledthread.com/blog/rags-to-stitches-the-kantha-work-of-west-bengal): a blog post with information about the history, plus examples and images of kantha embroidery
