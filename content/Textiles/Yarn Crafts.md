Yarn crafts take yarn of some quantity, texture, and color(s) and turn it into a fabric that may be garment-shaped, or that can be combined with another craft like [[Sewing]] to produce a garment or other object. The most widespread yarn crafts are [[Knitting]] and [[Crochet]], although there are other techniques like [[Tunesian Crochet]] and [[Nålbinding]].

[[Weaving]] is a very different yarn craft that always produces a sheet of fabric that needs to be sewn to take some other shape.

You can do yarn crafts with custom yarn created by [[Spinning]] to add extra dimensions of data.
