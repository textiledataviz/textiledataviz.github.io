Yarn crafts take yarn of some quantity, texture, and color(s) and turn it into a fabric that may be garment-shaped, or that can be combined with another craft like [[Textiles/Sewing/index|Sewing]] to produce a garment or other object. The most widespread yarn crafts are [[Textiles/Knitting/index|Knitting]] and [[Textiles/Crochet/index|Crochet]], although there are other techniques like [[Tunesian Crochet]] and [[Nålbinding]].

[[Textiles/Weaving/index|Weaving]] is a very different yarn craft that always produces a sheet of fabric that needs to be sewn to take some other shape.

You can do yarn crafts with custom yarn created by [[Textiles/Spinning/index|Spinning]] to add extra dimensions of data.
