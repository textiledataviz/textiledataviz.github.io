A pin loom is a (typically small) shape with small nails hammered in around the outside at regular intervals, used for [[Textiles/Weaving/index|weaving]]. The warp is wrapped around the nails, which produces a weaving in the same shape as the loom. Pin looms create [[Tapestry loom|tapestry weavings]] where the warp isn't visible.
