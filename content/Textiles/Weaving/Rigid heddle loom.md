A rigid heddle loom is a good option for beginners interested in producing medium-sized weavings (e.g. for scarves/shawls, or that can be pieced together with simple [[Garment Sewing]]). Rigid heddle looms produce balanced weavings, where both the warp and weft are visible in the final product.
